Access

By Train

  • If you use the Shinkansen (新幹線) express, the nearest station of Hirosaki is Shin-Aomori (新青森) station.
  • From Shin-Aomori (新青森) station, you can use the O-u Honsen (奥羽本線) train bound for Hirosaki (弘前) or Akita (秋田) station.
  • Please get off the train at Hirosaki (弘前) station.
  • Please note that you will need to pay an additional fee in the car if you use the Tsugaru express (特急つがる) or Shirakami resort express (リゾートしらかみ).

By Airplane

  • From Aomori airport, you can use an airport limousine bus to go to Hirosaki.
  • Please get off at Hirosaki station (弘前駅前) or Hirosaki bus terminal (弘前バスターミナル).

From Hirosaki station or bus terminal to Hirosaki University

  • It is a 25-minute walk or a 10-minute bus ride.
  • If you use a bus, take a bus of the Koguriyama-Oinomori (小栗山-狼森) or Gakuencho (学園町) line at Hirosaki station or bus terminal.
